What visual style should the cursor trail have?

This is the core look of your trail module. You can select more than one if you want the module to support multiple styles.

A

Line / Snake

A continuous smooth line that follows the cursor path. Can have gradient colors and variable thickness.

B

Particles

Small dots, stars, or shapes that spawn at the cursor and fade out. Great for sparkle or magic effects.

C

Ribbon

A smooth, thick ribbon with gradient colors that flows behind the cursor like a scarf.

D

Dots Trail

Discrete dots that appear at intervals behind the cursor and fade out over time. Simple and clean.

E

Glow

A soft, blurred glow that follows the cursor. Can be combined with any other style.
