What performance level do you need?

This affects how the trail is rendered and how much system resources it uses.

A

Minimal Footprint

Low CPU/GPU usage. Simple drawing, fewer particles. Good for older Macs or when running heavy apps. Recommended for a first version.

B

Balanced

Decent effects with reasonable resource usage. Some glow, some particles, but capped.

C

Maximum Quality

Full GPU-accelerated rendering, lots of particles, blur effects, 60fps. Higher battery usage.
